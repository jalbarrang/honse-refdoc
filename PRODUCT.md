# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

One person: the site's owner, a Umamusume: Pretty Derby player who built this as a personal reference. The typical context is mid-run — a training run is open on another screen, a decision is pending (a race choice, an event option, a stat threshold), and the site is opened to settle it. Occasionally the same person reads longer strategy articles between runs, but lookup under mild time pressure is the primary mode.

## Product Purpose

A searchable, statically prerendered reference site built from Erzzy and Kireina's 55,000-word Umamusume reference document, organized into six domain hubs with focused child articles and full-text search. It exists so the answer buried in a 4,463-line document is reachable in seconds instead of minutes. Success: someone with a mid-run question finds the answer in under a minute.

## Positioning

The fastest way to get an answer out of the refdoc — every screen exists to shorten the path from question to the relevant paragraph.

## Brand Personality

Quick, quietly characterful, calm. The site has a voice — a trackside field-guide sensibility inherited from its source material — but the voice stays in the margins. Tone down editorial drama in favor of speed and density: no theatrics between the reader and the answer.

## Anti-references

- Fandom/wiki clutter: ads-shaped layouts, boxes everywhere, dense competing sidebars.
- Generic docs-site chrome: the interchangeable Docusaurus/GitBook look.
- Gamer aesthetic: dark neon, HUD frames, anime gradients.

## Design Principles

- Answer speed beats atmosphere. When density and drama conflict, density wins.
- Character lives in the margins, never in the way. Personality shows in small touches, not in oversized set pieces the reader must scroll past.
- Scanning is the primary reading mode. Hierarchy, tables, and lists are tuned for finding one row, not for linear reading.
- Preserve the source faithfully. Authorial wording stays intact, and the CC BY-NC attribution to Erzzy and Kireina stays visible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA. Body text contrast at or above 4.5:1, keyboard-reachable navigation and search, visible focus states, and reduced-motion alternatives for every animation.
